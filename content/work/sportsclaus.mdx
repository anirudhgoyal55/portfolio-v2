A cricket platform built with an editorially-led founder who insisted on a zero-AI promise: every prediction reasoning is human-written, every dash earned. My role is the technical surface — daily dashboards, an internationalization pipeline, and the look-and-feel that makes the predictions feel earned.

## What's there now

- A D7 dashboard that surfaces the day's matches in a scoreboard register
- An i18n content pipeline for international audiences
- A design system that is bold and typographic, not Linear-quiet

## What's coming

Better signal on prediction reasoning. A shareable intel pass that travels well on Instagram. And a tighter loop between editor and dashboard so the platform's editorial voice carries through.
